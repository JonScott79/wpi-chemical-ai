About WPI Predict

Advancing molecular property prediction through machine learning.

WPI Predict is a research platform developed at Worcester Polytechnic Institute to provide fast, accurate, and accessible prediction of molecular properties using machine learning models and SMILES chemical representations.

Our Mission

WPI Predict aims to accelerate computational chemistry research by providing a unified platform for evaluating machine learning models that predict physical and thermodynamic molecular properties.

How It Works

Researchers enter a SMILES molecular representation, select a prediction model, and receive estimated molecular properties generated by experimentally trained machine learning models.

Technology

  • Machine Learning Models
  • SMILES Molecular Representations
  • Python Backend
  • Modern Web Interface
  • Extensible Model Architecture

Platform Goals

  • Support ongoing academic research.
  • Simplify molecular property prediction.
  • Provide a scalable platform for future models.
  • Encourage collaboration across research teams.
Research Platform

WPI Predict is actively developed as part of ongoing research at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. The platform is designed to support computational chemistry, cheminformatics, and machine learning research while remaining flexible enough to incorporate future prediction models as they are developed.
